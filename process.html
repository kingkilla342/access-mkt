Step by Step

Your Complete Journey

Every service is tailored to the client — no one-size-fits-all solutions here.

1
Getting Started

Create Your Account

Sign up for your secure ACCESS Marketplace account. You immediately access the client portal where your entire journey — documents, updates, and reports — is managed.

2
Secure Setup

Securely Upload Your Documents

Using our encrypted intake form, upload your required credit documentation. ACCESS uses bank-level encryption to protect your sensitive financial information.

🔒 All documents are encrypted in transit and at rest — SSL + PCI-compliant infrastructure.
3
Initial Consultation — $100

Schedule Your Expert Consultation

Book through the integrated calendar system. Pay securely and sign your electronic consultation agreement and disclosure forms before the session begins.

✦ Your $100 consultation fee is credited toward your service package upon enrollment.
4
Expert Review

Receive Your Expert Analysis

Within 48 hours of your consultation, a certified expert delivers a comprehensive written analysis of your credit file — identifying all disputable trade lines with a strategy tailored specifically to you.

5
Your Choice

Select Your Service Package

Based on your expert's recommendations, choose your service package. All packages include four full dispute rounds. Transparent per-trade-line pricing — no upsells, no hidden charges.

📊 1–3 ($500) · 4–6 ($1,000) · 7–12 ($1,500) · 13–18 ($2,000) · 19+ ($2,500)
6
Official Enrollment

Complete the On-Boarding Process

Finalize your service agreement with an electronic signature, make your initial deposit, and complete all CROA compliance disclosures including your privacy acknowledgment and no-guarantee disclaimer.

7
Active Dispute Phase

4 Rounds of Structured Disputes

Your ACCESS expert performs at least four formal dispute rounds on your behalf, communicating directly with Equifax, Experian, and TransUnion. Each round uses strategic letters backed by documentation and federal law.

⚡ You don't contact any bureaus — we manage the entire process on your behalf from start to finish.
8
Stay Informed

Receive Monthly Updates

Throughout the dispute process you'll receive monthly status reports via your client portal. Track each trade line's progress, review bureau correspondence, and communicate with your ACCESS team at any time.

9
Completion

Receive Your Final Summary Report

Upon completion of all four rounds, you'll receive a comprehensive Final Summary Report — every dispute, every outcome, and every credit improvement documented. Yours to keep as a permanent record.

🏆 Your improved score, full dispute documentation, and next steps for long-term financial health.
